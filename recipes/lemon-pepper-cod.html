Lemon Pepper Cod

Description

This simple yet delicious recipe showcases the delicate flavor of cod. The fillets are cooked in a pan, infused with the bright acidity of lemon juice and a subtle hint of black pepper. This combination creates a harmonious balance of flavors that is both refreshing and satisfying.

To complement the cod, serve it with a buttery rice pilaf for a comforting and hearty side dish. The creamy texture of the rice and the rich flavor of the butter perfectly complement the flaky fish. For a touch of freshness and added nutrients, pair the meal with a green salad. The crisp vegetables and light dressing will help to balance the richness of the other dishes, creating a well-rounded and enjoyable meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 Â½ pounds cod fillets
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium high heat until hot.
  2. Sprinkle with pepper to taste.
  3. Add fillets and squeeze 1/2 of the lemon's juice over the tops.ook for 4 minutes and turn.
  4. Squeeze with the remaining lemon's juice and sprinkle with pepper to taste.
  5. Continue to cook until fillets flake easily with a fork.
